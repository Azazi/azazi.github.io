Alaa Azazi

Alaa Azazi worked as a graduate research assistant at the Agile Surface Engineering Group at the University of Calgary. He received his B.Sc. in Computer Science & General Mathematics and his M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Calgary in 2014 and 2016 respectively. His work spans areas of ubiquitous computing, human-computer interaction, and software engineering.

Alaa is most interested in exploring novel technologies and approaches, and enhancing existing implementations for achieving spatial-awareness and accurate tracking in ubiquitous environments.
