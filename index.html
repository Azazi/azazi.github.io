About

Building systems that scale.

I'm a Lead Software Engineer with 10+ years of experience architecting large-scale distributed systems and data platforms. My work spans cloud-native architecture, event-driven microservices, and engineering leadership — with a consistent focus on systems that are reliable, observable, and built to evolve.

At Datavant I lead platform, ingestion, and infrastructure teams building the health-data connectivity layer that processes 10B+ records per day — handling the security, accessibility, and interoperability of sensitive health data at scale. Previously at AWS I owned the architecture for Migration Service assessment pipelines. Across both, my emphasis has been on operational excellence: SLOs, observability, API governance, and growing engineers into technical leaders.

I thrive at the intersection of technical depth and organizational strategy — turning architectural decisions into measurable outcomes, and building teams capable of owning and evolving complex systems independently.
